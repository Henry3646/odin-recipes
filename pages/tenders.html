Spicy Chicken Tenders

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Corn Flakes
  • Franks Red Hot
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Chili Powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions:

  1. Cut Chicken into desired size and shape
  2. Smother that chicken with your Franks Red Hot
  3. Add your desired amounts of the spices listed in Ingredients, then mix
  4. Pour corn flakes onto a pan or plate, then crush them upp into tiny pieces
    (but not to a fine powder, we still want some crunch in there)
  5. Cover your Chicken in the corn flakes and set aside
  6. Spray not stick spray into your Air Fryer, then place chicken into the air fryer
  7. Cook at 400 degrees Fahrenheit for 18 minutes, flipping half way through
  8. Enjoy!
