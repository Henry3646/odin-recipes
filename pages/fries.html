Parmesan French Fries

image coming soon (next time i make them)

Ingredients:

  • French Fries
  • Garlic Salt
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Ranch Seasoning
  • Pepper
  • Parmesan Cheese

Directions:

  1. Put French fries into a bowl
  2. Mix desired quantities of spices into a tiny bowl and add to fries, then mix fries in bowl
  3. Cook fries in Air Fryer for 8 mins at 400 degrees Fahrenheit, shaking half way through
  4. Once finished cooking, add parmesan cheese and shake
  5. Enjoy!
